Streaming App Installation Fixes – Technical Guide

This page covers technical fixes for streaming app installation problems on Smart TVs, Fire TV, Roku, Android TV, and mobile devices.

For Peacock TV–specific instructions, you can reference the main help page: Peacock TV Installation & Troubleshooting – Independent Help

1. App won’t install or gets stuck at “Pending”

  1. Check available storage on your device and free up space if it’s low.
  2. Restart the device (TV, stick, box, or phone) and try the installation again.
  3. On Android/Google TV, clear cache & data of the Google Play Store or system app store.
  4. Ensure your internet is stable (switch from Wi-Fi to wired where possible).
  5. Sign out and sign back into the device’s app store account, then retry.

2. App installs but won’t open

  1. Force stop the app and relaunch it.
  2. Uninstall and reinstall the app completely.
  3. Update the device firmware / OS to the latest available version.
  4. Disable any VPN/Proxy and test again.
  5. Try installing a different streaming app to see if the issue is app-specific.

3. Region / compatibility problems

  1. Confirm the app is officially available in your country’s app store.
  2. Check that your device model is supported by the streaming app.
  3. Remove and re-add any DNS/content-filtering services from your router and test again.
